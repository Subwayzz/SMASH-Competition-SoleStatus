Product Information

Here you can find information about our products designed to help manage diabetes.


SoleStatus is a pressure monitoring software that reduces lower-limb amputations by preventing foot ulcers in diabetes patients. Contributing to UNSDG target #3.4, SoleStatus places a high priority on accessibility.
